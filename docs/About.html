Empowering Kenya with Quality Electricals & Electronics for Over 25 Years

Who We Are

ALPHAGEN COMPANY.LTD, operating under the brand name HEYZ ELECTRICALS AND ELECTRONICS, is a leading supplier of quality electrical and electronic products in Kenya. Our journey began over 25 years ago, and since then, we have been committed to delivering innovation, reliability, and value to our customers.

Our main shop is strategically located in Trans Nzoia County, Kitale town, right opposite the County Revenue Offices. We also operate multiple branches across different counties in Kenya to ensure our products are accessible to as many customers as possible.

We take pride in our experienced team, excellent customer service, and dedication to quality. From home appliances and lighting solutions to industrial electronics and smart devices, we provide trusted solutions for homes, businesses, and institutions.
